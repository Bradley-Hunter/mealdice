method: [air_fryer]
ingredients:
  - flatbread
  - olive oil
  - minced garlic
  - cheddar cheese
  - italian seasoning
  - salt
description: Flatbread with garlic oil and melted cheese.
servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Mix 1 tablespoon olive oil with 1 teaspoon minced garlic and pinch of salt.
2. Brush over a flatbread.
3. Top with cheddar cheese and Italian seasoning.
4. Air fry at 350°F for 3-4 minutes.
