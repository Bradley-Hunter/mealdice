  - tomato sauce

  - garlic powder
  - cheddar cheese
  - salt
description: Classic weeknight spaghetti.
servings: 2
---

## Steps

1. Boil salted water. Cook spaghetti 10 minutes. Drain.
2. Return to pot. Stir in tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, garlic powder.
3. Top with shredded cheddar cheese.
