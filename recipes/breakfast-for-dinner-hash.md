  - olive oil

  - pepper
  - garlic powder
  - ranch dressing
description: Crispy diced potatoes and onion with an egg on top.
servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Dice 2-3 potatoes into 1/4 to 1/2 inch cubes.
2. Dice 1/2 yellow onion to match.
3. Toss with olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder.
4. Air fry at 400°F for 15-18 minutes, shake halfway.
5. Fry 1-2 eggs in a skillet.
6. Plate hash, top with egg, cheese, ranch.
