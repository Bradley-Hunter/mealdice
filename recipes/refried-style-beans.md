servings: 2
---

## Steps

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet. Add 1/2 diced yellow onion, cook 3 minutes.
2. Add 1 teaspoon minced garlic, cook 30 seconds.
3. Add 1.5 cups cooked black beans plus 1/4 cup water.
4. Mash with a fork or potato masher until mostly smooth.
5. Cook 2-3 minutes until thickened. Salt and garlic powder to taste.
