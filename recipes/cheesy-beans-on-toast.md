## Steps

1. Toast a flatbread in a skillet or air fryer 2-3 minutes until crisp.
2. Warm 1 cup cooked black beans with 2 tablespoons water, garlic powder, salt.
3. Mash slightly for a chunky sauce.
4. Pile onto the toasted flatbread.
5. Top with cheddar cheese.
