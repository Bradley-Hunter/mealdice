## Steps

1. Heat a skillet on medium with a splash of olive oil.
2. Crack 1-2 eggs into the skillet. Cook to preferred doneness.
3. Warm a flatbread in the microwave 15 seconds or toast briefly in a dry skillet.
4. Top the flatbread with the egg and shredded cheddar cheese.
5. Salt and pepper to taste.
