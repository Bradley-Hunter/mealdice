  - cheddar cheese
  - flatbread
  - ranch dressing

servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Mash 1/2 cup cooked black beans with a fork.
2. Spread across a flatbread.
3. Sprinkle cheddar cheese.
4. Roll up tightly.
5. Optional: air fry at 350°F for 3 minutes.
6. Dunk in ranch.
