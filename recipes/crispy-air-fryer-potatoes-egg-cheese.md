## Steps

1. Scrub and dice 2-3 russet potatoes into 1/4 to 1/2 inch cubes.
2. Toss with 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
3. Air fry at 400°F for 12-18 minutes, shaking halfway.
4. Near the end, fry 1-2 eggs in a skillet.
5. Last 2 minutes of air frying, sprinkle cheddar cheese over the potatoes to melt.
6. Plate, top with egg, drizzle ranch.
