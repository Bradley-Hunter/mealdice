servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Scrub 1-2 russet potatoes. Poke holes with a fork.
2. Microwave 5 minutes. Flip. Microwave 3-5 more minutes.
3. Slice open lengthwise, fluff with a fork.
4. Top with cheddar cheese, cooked black beans, ranch dressing. Salt and pepper.
