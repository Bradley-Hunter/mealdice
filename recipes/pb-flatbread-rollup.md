ingredients:
  - flatbread
  - peanut butter
  - brown sugar
description: Pre-made flatbread as a wrap for peanut butter. Zero cooking.
servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Take one homemade flatbread.
2. Spread peanut butter across it edge to edge.
3. Sprinkle brown sugar over the peanut butter.
4. Roll up and eat like a wrap.
