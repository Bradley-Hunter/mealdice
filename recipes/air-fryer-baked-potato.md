servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Scrub 1-2 russet potatoes. Poke holes with a fork.
2. Rub with olive oil and salt.
3. Air fry at 400°F for 35-45 minutes until soft when squeezed.
4. Slice open, top with cheddar cheese and ranch.
