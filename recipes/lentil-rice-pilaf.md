## Steps

1. Heat olive oil in a pot. Add 1 diced yellow onion, cook 5-7 minutes until deeply golden.
2. Add 1 teaspoon minced garlic, cook 30 seconds.
3. Add 1/2 cup lentils, 3/4 cup jasmine rice, 2.5 cups water, 1.5 teaspoons bouillon powder, salt.
4. Bring to a boil, reduce to low, cover, cook 20-25 minutes until liquid absorbed.
5. Fluff with fork.
