  - jasmine rice
  - soy sauce
  - minced garlic
  - olive oil
  - eggs
description: Rice cooked plain, then stir-fried with soy sauce and garlic.
servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Cook 1/2 cup jasmine rice with 3/4 cup water, boil, cover, reduce to low 15 minutes.
2. Heat olive oil in a skillet. Add 1 teaspoon minced garlic, cook 30 seconds.
3. Add cooked rice, toss with 1-2 tablespoons soy sauce.
4. Optional: fry an egg on top.
