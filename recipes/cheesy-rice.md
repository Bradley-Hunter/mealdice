servings: 2
---

## Steps

1. Heat a splash of olive oil in a pot. Add 1 cup jasmine rice, stir to coat.
2. Add 1.5 cups water, 1 teaspoon bouillon powder, pinch of salt.
3. Bring to a boil, reduce to low, cover, cook 15 minutes.
4. Off heat, stir in shredded cheddar cheese until melted.
