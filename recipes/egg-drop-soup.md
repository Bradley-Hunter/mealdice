servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Heat olive oil in a small pot. Add 1/4 diced yellow onion, cook 2 minutes.
2. Add 1 cup water and 1 teaspoon bouillon powder. Bring to a simmer.
3. Beat 1 egg in a bowl. Slowly drizzle into the simmering broth while stirring.
4. Add 1 teaspoon soy sauce.
5. Pour over 1 cup cooked rice.
