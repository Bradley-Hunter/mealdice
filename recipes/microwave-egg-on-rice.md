ingredients:
  - cooked rice
  - eggs
  - soy sauce
  - cheddar cheese
description: Ultra-fast bowl with pre-cooked rice.
servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Scoop 1 cup cooked rice into a bowl. Microwave 60 seconds.
2. Crack an egg onto the rice. Poke the yolk with a fork once or twice.
3. Microwave 40-60 seconds until egg is set.
4. Drizzle soy sauce, sprinkle cheddar cheese.
