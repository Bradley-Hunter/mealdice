## Steps

1. Crack 2 eggs into a large microwave-safe mug.
2. Add a splash of water. Whisk with a fork.
3. Microwave 60 seconds. Stir. Microwave 30 more seconds.
4. Stir in shredded cheddar cheese. Salt and pepper.
