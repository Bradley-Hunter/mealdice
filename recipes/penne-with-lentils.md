method: [stove]
ingredients:
  - penne pasta
  - cooked lentils
  - olive oil
  - minced garlic
  - italian seasoning
  - cheddar cheese
  - salt
description: Pasta tossed with pre-cooked lentils for extra protein.
servings: 2
---

## Steps

1. Cook penne in salted boiling water 10 minutes. Drain.
2. In the pot, heat olive oil. Add 1 teaspoon minced garlic, cook 30 seconds.
3. Add cooked lentils, pasta, 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning, salt. Toss.
4. Top with cheddar cheese.
