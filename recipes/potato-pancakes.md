## Steps

1. Grate 2 russet potatoes and 1/4 yellow onion into a bowl (or finely dice if no grater).
2. Squeeze out excess water.
3. Mix in 1 egg, 2 tablespoons flour, salt, pepper.
4. Heat olive oil in a skillet on medium. Drop small patties in, flatten.
5. Cook 3-4 minutes per side until golden and crisp.
