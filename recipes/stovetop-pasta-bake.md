  - penne pasta
  - tomato sauce
  - diced tomatoes
  - italian seasoning
  - cheddar cheese
  - salt
description: Cooked pasta mixed with sauce, topped with melted cheese.
servings: 2
---

## Steps

1. Boil salted water. Cook penne 10 minutes. Drain.
2. Mix pasta with tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, Italian seasoning in an oven-safe dish.
3. Top with cheddar cheese.
4. Air fry at 350°F for 5-7 minutes until golden.
