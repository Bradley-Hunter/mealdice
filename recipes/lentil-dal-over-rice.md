## Steps

1. Start jasmine rice in a pot (1 cup rice to 1.5 cups water, boil, cover, reduce to low 15 minutes).
2. Rinse 1 cup lentils.
3. In another pot, heat olive oil on medium. Add 1/2 diced yellow onion, cook 3-4 minutes.
4. Add 1 teaspoon minced garlic and 1 teaspoon curry powder. Stir 30 seconds.
5. Add lentils, 2.5 cups water, 1 teaspoon bouillon powder, pinch of salt.
6. Simmer uncovered 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
7. Serve over rice.
