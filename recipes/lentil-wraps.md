## Steps

1. Warm cooked lentil dal in the microwave 30-60 seconds.
2. Spoon 1/2 cup dal onto a flatbread.
3. Sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese.
4. Roll up tightly.
5. Optional: air fry at 350°F for 2-3 minutes to crisp.
