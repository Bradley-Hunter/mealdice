---
name: Potato and Egg Breakfast Bowl
meal_types: [breakfast]
prep_time_min: 15
method: [microwave, stove]
ingredients:
  - russet potato
  - eggs
  - cheddar cheese
  - olive oil
  - salt
  - pepper
  - garlic powder
description: Microwaved potato diced up with a scrambled egg on top.
servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Scrub and poke 1 russet potato. Microwave 5 minutes, flip, microwave 3-4 more minutes until soft.
2. Let cool slightly, then dice into rough cubes.
3. Heat a skillet with olive oil on medium. Add the potato cubes, season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Cook 3-4 minutes until edges crisp.
4. Scramble 2 eggs into the skillet, mixing with the potatoes.
5. Off heat, top with shredded cheddar cheese.
