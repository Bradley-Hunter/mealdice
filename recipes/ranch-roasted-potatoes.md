  - ranch dressing

  - salt
  - pepper
  - garlic powder

servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Dice 2-3 russet potatoes.
2. Toss with 2 tablespoons ranch dressing, a drizzle of olive oil, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
3. Air fry at 400°F for 16-18 minutes, shaking halfway.
