method: [stove]
ingredients:
  - spaghetti pasta
  - minced garlic
  - olive oil
  - cheddar cheese
  - salt
  - pepper
description: Minimalist pasta with garlic, oil, and cheese.
servings: 2
---

## Steps

1. Boil salted water. Cook spaghetti 10 minutes. Reserve 1/4 cup pasta water, drain.
2. In the same pot, heat 3 tablespoons olive oil on low. Add 1 tablespoon minced garlic. Cook 1 minute — don't brown.
3. Return pasta. Toss with the garlic oil and a splash of pasta water.
4. Top with cheddar cheese, salt, pepper.
