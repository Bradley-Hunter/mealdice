## Steps

1. Sprinkle cheddar cheese on half of a flatbread.
2. Optional: add cooked black beans over the cheese.
3. Fold in half.
4. Cook in a dry skillet on medium, 2 minutes per side.
5. Or air fry at 350°F for 3-4 minutes.
