  - brown sugar

servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Put 1/2 cup rolled oats and 1 cup water in a large microwave-safe bowl.
2. Microwave on high for 2 minutes.
3. Stir in brown sugar to taste. Add a pinch of salt if you want the sweetness to pop.
