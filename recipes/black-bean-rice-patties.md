---

## Steps

1. Mash 1.5 cups cooked black beans in a bowl.
2. Mix in 1/2 cup cooked rice, 1 egg, 3 tablespoons flour, 2 tablespoons finely diced yellow onion, 1 teaspoon garlic powder, salt.
3. Form into 4-5 patties.
4. Heat olive oil in a skillet on medium. Cook patties 3-4 minutes per side until golden.
5. Serve with ranch or plain.
