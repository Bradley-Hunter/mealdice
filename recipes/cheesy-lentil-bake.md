## Steps

1. Mix 1 cup cooked lentils with 1/2 can tomato sauce, 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning, pinch of salt in an air-fryer-safe dish.
2. Top generously with cheddar cheese.
3. Air fry at 350°F for 8-10 minutes until cheese is golden and bubbly.
