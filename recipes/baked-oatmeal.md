## Steps

1. In a bowl, mix 1 cup rolled oats, 1 egg, 2 tablespoons brown sugar, 1 tablespoon olive oil, a pinch of salt, and 1/2 cup water.
2. Pour into a small air-fryer-safe dish (like a ramekin or small cake pan).
3. Air fry at 325°F for 15-18 minutes until set and golden on top.
4. Let cool 2 minutes before eating. Keeps in the fridge for next-day breakfast.
