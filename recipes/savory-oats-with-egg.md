## Steps

1. Put 1/2 cup rolled oats, 1 cup water, and 1 teaspoon bouillon powder in a large microwave-safe bowl.
2. Microwave 2 minutes. Stir.
3. Fry an egg in a skillet with a splash of olive oil.
4. Stir shredded cheddar cheese into the oats until melted.
5. Top with the fried egg. Salt and pepper to taste.
