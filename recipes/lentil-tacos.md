## Steps

1. Heat olive oil in a pot. Cook 1/2 diced yellow onion 3 minutes.
2. Add 1 teaspoon minced garlic, cook 30 seconds.
3. Add 3/4 cup lentils, 1 can diced tomatoes, 1.5 cups water, 1 teaspoon garlic powder, 1 teaspoon onion powder, pinch of salt.
4. Simmer 20 minutes until lentils soft and liquid thick.
5. Spoon into flatbreads with cheddar cheese.
