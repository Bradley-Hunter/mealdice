  - eggs

  - soy sauce
  - olive oil
  - cooked black beans
description: Stir-fried leftover rice with egg and onion.
servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet on medium-high.
2. Add 1/2 diced yellow onion. Cook 2-3 minutes.
3. Add 1.5 cups cooked rice. Toss to coat.
4. Push rice to one side. Crack 1-2 eggs into empty side. Scramble, then mix in.
5. Optional: add cooked black beans.
6. Drizzle soy sauce, toss.
