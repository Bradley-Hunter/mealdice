ingredients:
  - flatbread
  - olive oil
  - garlic powder
  - salt
description: Flatbread cut into chips and air-fried. Great with ranch.
servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Cut 2 flatbreads into triangles or strips.
2. Toss with 1 tablespoon olive oil, garlic powder, salt.
3. Air fry at 375°F for 5-6 minutes until crisp.
