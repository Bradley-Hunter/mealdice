## Steps

1. Dice 2-3 russet potatoes into 1/2 inch cubes.
2. Toss with olive oil, 1 teaspoon curry powder, garlic powder, onion powder, salt.
3. Air fry at 400°F for 18-20 minutes, shake halfway.
4. Serve with ranch.
