servings: 2
---

## Steps

1. Dice 2 russet potatoes and 1/2 yellow onion.
2. Heat olive oil in a pot. Cook onion 3 minutes.
3. Add 1 teaspoon minced garlic, cook 30 seconds.
4. Add potatoes, 1.5 cups water, 1.5 teaspoons bouillon powder.
5. Simmer 15-20 minutes until potatoes are soft.
6. Mash half the potatoes in the pot to thicken.
7. Stir in shredded cheddar cheese off heat. Salt and pepper.
