ingredients:
  - flatbread
  - cooked black beans
  - cheddar cheese
  - ranch dressing
  - salt
  - garlic powder
description: Crispy flatbread base topped with beans and cheese.
servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Air fry a flatbread at 400°F for 3 minutes until crisp.
2. Mash 1/2 cup cooked black beans, season with salt and garlic powder.
3. Spread beans over the crispy flatbread.
4. Top with shredded cheddar cheese.
5. Air fry another 3-4 minutes until cheese melts.
6. Drizzle ranch.
