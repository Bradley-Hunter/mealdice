## Steps

1. Rinse 1/2 cup lentils.
2. Heat olive oil in pot. Add 1/2 diced yellow onion, cook 3 minutes.
3. Add 1 teaspoon minced garlic, cook 30 seconds.
4. Add lentils, 1 can diced tomatoes, 1 can tomato sauce, 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning, pinch of salt, 1 cup water.
5. Simmer 20-25 minutes until lentils are soft.
6. Meanwhile, cook penne pasta 10 minutes. Drain.
7. Serve sauce over pasta. Top with cheddar cheese.
