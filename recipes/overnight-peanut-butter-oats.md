## Steps

1. The night before: combine 1/2 cup rolled oats, 1 cup water, a spoonful of peanut butter, and a spoonful of brown sugar in a jar or bowl.
2. Stir, cover, refrigerate overnight.
3. Morning: stir and eat cold, or microwave 60 seconds if you want warm.
