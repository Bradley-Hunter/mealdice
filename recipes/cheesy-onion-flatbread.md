  - flatbread
  - yellow onion
  - cheddar cheese
  - olive oil
  - salt
description: Sweet caramelized onions on flatbread with cheese.
servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Slice 1 yellow onion thinly.
2. Heat olive oil in a skillet on medium-low. Add onion and a pinch of salt.
3. Cook 12-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until deep golden and sweet.
4. Spread onions on a flatbread. Top with cheddar cheese.
5. Air fry at 350°F for 3-4 minutes until cheese melts.
