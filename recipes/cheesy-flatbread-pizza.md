ingredients:
  - flatbread
  - tomato sauce
  - italian seasoning
  - cheddar cheese
description: Flatbread as pizza crust, air-fried until bubbly.
servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Spread thin layer of tomato sauce on a flatbread.
2. Sprinkle Italian seasoning.
3. Cover with shredded cheddar cheese.
4. Air fry at 350°F for 4-5 minutes.
