servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Cut 2 russet potatoes into wedges (halve, then quarter each half).
2. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a skillet on medium.
3. Place wedges cut-side down. Cook 4-5 minutes per cut side until golden.
4. Cover with a lid, reduce to medium-low, cook 6-8 more minutes until tender inside.
5. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder. Dip in ranch.
