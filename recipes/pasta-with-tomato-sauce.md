## Steps

1. Boil salted water. Cook 1 box penne pasta 10 minutes. Drain.
2. Return to pot. Stir in 1 can tomato sauce, 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning, garlic powder.
3. Top with shredded cheddar cheese.
