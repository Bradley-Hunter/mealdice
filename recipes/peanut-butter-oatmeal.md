  - brown sugar

servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Put 1/2 cup rolled oats and 1 cup water in a large microwave-safe bowl. Use a bigger bowl than you think you need — oats foam up.
2. Microwave on high for 2 minutes.
3. Stir in a heaping spoonful of peanut butter until it melts in.
4. Stir in brown sugar to taste.
