  - cheddar cheese
  - italian seasoning
  - salt
description: Cold pasta with ranch dressing — stores great for leftovers.
servings: 2
---

## Steps

1. Boil salted water. Cook penne 10 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.
2. Finely dice 1/4 yellow onion.
3. Toss pasta with ranch dressing, diced onion, Italian seasoning, cubed cheddar cheese.
4. Eat cold or room temp. Keeps in fridge 3 days.
