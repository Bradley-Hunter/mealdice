  - cooked black beans

  - garlic powder
  - ranch dressing

servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Scoop cooked rice into a bowl. Microwave 60 seconds.
2. Add cooked black beans. Microwave 30-45 more seconds.
3. Top with shredded cheddar cheese — heat melts it.
4. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder.
5. Optional: drizzle ranch dressing.
