ingredients:
  - penne pasta
  - cheddar cheese
  - olive oil
  - flour
  - salt
  - garlic powder
description: No-roux cheesy pasta using pasta water as the sauce base.
servings: 2
---

## Steps

1. Cook penne in salted boiling water 10 minutes. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water. Drain.
2. In the pot, heat 1 tablespoon olive oil on low. Whisk in 1 tablespoon flour, cook 1 minute.
3. Slowly whisk in reserved pasta water until smooth.
4. Off heat, stir in 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese and pasta. Garlic powder and salt to taste.
