  - eggs

  - olive oil
  - salt
  - pepper

servings: 1
---

## Steps

1. Crack 2 eggs into a bowl. Beat with a fork until yolks break up.
2. Heat a skillet on medium with a splash of olive oil.
3. Pour eggs in. Let sit 20 seconds.
4. Push gently with a spatula until mostly set but still glossy.
5. Off heat, add shredded cheddar cheese and let melt. Salt and pepper.
