## Steps

1. Dice 2 potatoes. Dice 1/2 yellow onion.
2. Heat olive oil in a pot. Cook onion 3 minutes.
3. Add 1 teaspoon minced garlic, 1.5 teaspoons curry powder. Stir 30 seconds.
4. Add potatoes, 1/2 cup lentils, 1 can diced tomatoes, 1.5 cups water, 1 teaspoon bouillon powder, salt.
5. Simmer 25-30 minutes until potatoes and lentils are tender.
6. Serve over rice or with flatbread.
